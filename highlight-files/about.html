About Me

Pavel Sanchez recently earned an Associate of Applied Science Degree in Graphic Design from the Art Institute of New York City. While completing his studies, he interend for DailyMail as a graphic designer.

From a young age I have always been fascinated by beautiful handwritting. In high school I got a change to take a calligraphy class, which increased my love for calligraphy and lettering. While in high school, I taught myself to use photoshop so I could create banners and wallpapers using my calligraphy. I also taught myself HTML and CSS, to create websites. After HS I entered college to study computer engineering. While there I realized that I wanted to do more of the design of the websites than creating programs. This led me to join the Marines after 1 year of school.

While a Marine, I was stattioned in San Diego, Mississippi, Georgia, and Iraq. The Marines taught me the values of hard work, commitment, and courage. Along with the skills of time, project, and people management. It also taught me to take value and respect in what I do and what others do. Skills and value that I apply to everything I do.

On my free time I like to read, watch movies and go to concerts. Music is probably the biggest part of my life and the one thing I could not live without.
